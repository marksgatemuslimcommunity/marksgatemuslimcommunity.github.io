About us

Marks Gate Muslim Community was established in 2013, by some local brothers. Initially it was Jummah for the growing Muslim community and then other services were added to cater for the needs and demands of the community. We currently organise Jummah, Tarawih, Eid Salah, Children's madrasah, and other community events. We have an interim committee made up of 10 volunteers who live locally and organise the Islamic activities.


Regular meetings take place where we discuss matters and organise our activities.


We are a growing Muslim community and welcome contributions (financial and volunteers).
