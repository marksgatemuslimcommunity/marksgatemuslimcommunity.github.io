As-salamu alaykum and Welcome to Marks Gate Muslim Community!

What we do

We provide facilities for prayer particularly for Jummah prayer as well as Taraweeh prayer during Ramadan.

Is that all we do?

We provide fund raising for those unfortunate in life (Poor and needy). We also provide Madrasah classes for children being taught by an experienced Mualim.

What is our goal?

  • Continuous Islamic events
  • Development of local community
  • Contribute to social, spirital and economic enhancement of the whole community

We plan to do so much more
